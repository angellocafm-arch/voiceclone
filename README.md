# VoiceClone — Preserva tu voz. Para siempre.

> Open source voice cloning + personality AI for people with ALS and speech disabilities

---

## 🎯 What is VoiceClone?

VoiceClone is a **three-layer open source platform** that lets anyone preserve and use their voice, with a special focus on people with **ALS, ELA, and similar conditions that take away your voice**.

### The Problem
- **ElevenLabs costs €330/month**
- **There's no tool designed for people losing their voice**
- **AAC software doesn't integrate personalized voices**
- **Your voice data shouldn't go to cloud servers**

### The Solution
**3 Integrated Layers:**

1. **Capa 1 — Voice Cloning Engine** (Local, Open Source)
   - Clone your voice in 2-3 minutes
   - 100% local. 100% private. 100% yours.
   - Uses Chatterbox TTS (MIT, SOTA quality)

2. **Capa 2 — Accessible Web App** (For ELA + Disabilities)
   - Designed for eye tracking (Tobii SDK)
   - WCAG AA compliant
   - Integrates with AAC software (Grid 3, Proloquo2Go)

3. **Capa 3 — Personality AI** (Your Voice, Your Words)
   - Not just your voice: your personality
   - LLM learns how you speak
   - Generates text as YOU would say it

---

## ✨ Key Features

- ✅ **One command install** — Download, install, done (no terminal needed)
- ✅ **Zero-shot voice cloning** — 5 seconds of audio = your voice
- ✅ **Completely local** — Nothing leaves your computer
- ✅ **Accessible design** — Eye tracking, switch control, keyboard navigation
- ✅ **Personality preservation** — Your voice + your way of speaking
- ✅ **AAC integration** — Use with Grid 3, Proloquo2Go, etc.
- ✅ **Open source MIT** — Forever free, no paywalls

## 🚀 Quick Start

### MVP (Phase 3) — Coming Soon

**macOS:**
```bash
curl -fsSL https://voiceclone.dev/install | bash
```

**After installation:**
1. Open VoiceClone app
2. Click "Clone My Voice"
3. Record 2-3 minutes (guided)
4. Done! Your voice is ready
