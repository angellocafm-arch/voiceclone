# VoiceClone v0.3.0 — First Public Release

## 🎤 Tu voz, para siempre | Your voice, forever

VoiceClone es un asistente de vida completo para personas con ELA y dificultades motoras. Clona tu voz con solo 5 segundos de audio y la convierte en tu herramienta para seguir comunicándote.

**100% gratis. 100% privado. 100% local.**

---

## ✨ What's included

- 💬 **Communication** — Speak with your cloned voice via a phrase board or free text
- 🖥️ **Computer Control** — Give natural language commands to your computer
- 📝 **Productivity** — Dictate documents, read PDFs aloud, manage agenda
- 📱 **Messaging** — Send and receive Telegram messages with your voice
- 🎤 **Voice Cloning** — Clone your voice with just 5 seconds of audio (Chatterbox TTS)
- 🧠 **Local AI** — All processing happens on your computer (Ollama + Llama/Mistral)

## 📦 Installation

### macOS / Linux
```bash
curl -fsSL https://github.com/angellocafm-arch/voiceclone/releases/latest/download/install.sh | bash
```

### Windows (PowerShell as Administrator)
```powershell
irm https://github.com/angellocafm-arch/voiceclone/releases/latest/download/install_windows.ps1 | iex
```

## 💻 Requirements

- **RAM:** 4GB minimum, 8GB+ recommended
- **Disk:** 5GB free space
- **OS:** macOS 12+, Linux (Ubuntu 20.04+, Debian 11+, Fedora 36+), Windows 10/11
- **Internet:** Only needed during installation (downloads ~4GB of models)

## 🔒 Privacy

- No cloud, no accounts, no subscriptions
- Your voice never leaves your computer
- Zero telemetry
- Open source (MIT License)

## ⚠️ Known Limitations (v0.3.0)

- Frontend web app is still in development
- WhatsApp and Signal channels are in progress
- Voice cloning quality depends on reference audio quality
- First launch is slow (model download + initialization)
